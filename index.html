Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas Holds 2017 Awards Ceremony and Appreciation Lunch for Stakeholders in Region XI

09.08.2017

In celebration of its 24th anniversary, the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP) will hold the 2017 Awards Ceremony and Appreciation for BSP stakeholders in Region XI who have continuously supported its statistical initiatives, information requirements, advocacy programs as well as the effective delivery of its functions.
